Life at FUEL

Why Practitioners
Choose FUEL

More than a workplace — a community of practitioners committed to doing right by every patient.

Collaborative Culture

Practitioners here talk to each other — about patients, about techniques, about growth. You'll never feel like you're treating in a silo.

Modern Facility

A fully equipped, purpose-built clinic on 124 Street NW. Clean, professional, and designed for both practitioner efficiency and patient comfort.

Growing Practice

FUEL is an award-winning, expanding clinic with a strong and loyal patient base — giving you stability, volume, and a platform to grow your caseload.

Interdisciplinary Approach

Work alongside chiropractors, osteopaths, acupuncturists, psychologists, and personal trainers. The cross-discipline learning here is unmatched.

Competitive Compensation

We offer competitive pay structures that reflect your experience and value — because we want to attract and retain the best practitioners in Edmonton.

Patient-First Mission

Every decision at FUEL is made with patient outcomes in mind. You'll work somewhere your values and your work are fully aligned.
