Meet the FUEL Team

8 practitioners. One clinic. Every discipline you need to recover faster, move better, and feel like yourself again.

Collaborative Team

Brandon Barr

Owner & Manual Osteopath
MOT · RMT · PTS

A former collegiate elite athlete, Brandon has spent nearly two decades integrating therapies to reduce recovery time. Trained at Grant MacEwan University and the National Manual Osteopathic College of Canada, he is currently pursuing a Graduate Certificate in Pain Management at the University of Alberta. Outside the clinic, you'll find him playing baseball, hockey, golf, or walking his dog.

Saša (Sasha) Babović

Chiropractor
BSc Kinesiology · DC · MSc Sports Medicine

A former youth sports coach with deep biomechanics expertise, Sasha graduated from Simon Fraser University before completing his Doctor of Chiropractic and a Master of Science in Sports Medicine from the University of Western States. He emphasizes patient education and individualized care through collaborative, multi-angle treatment approaches. Enjoys sports, golf, gaming, and the sauna.

Christina Worthing

Psychologist
Registered Psychologist

Christina specializes in pain management through biopsychosocial models, using ACT, CBT, Solution-Focused, and Emotion-Focused therapy. She addresses anxiety, stress, depression, grief, relationships, sleep, and chronic pain. Outside the clinic, Christina plays music at church, cycles, hikes, and plays board games.

Zihao (Albert) Wang

Doctor of Acupuncture & RMT
DAc · TCM Practitioner · RMT

Albert combines traditional Chinese medicine techniques with contemporary approaches to deliver holistic care. Fluent in English and Mandarin, he offers needling, cupping, moxibustion, and ear seed therapy — tailoring each session to the individual. His bilingual practice makes quality TCM care accessible to a wider community.

Chase Dach

International Osteopath & RMT
DOMP · RMT

With European osteopathy training and MacEwan massage therapy credentials, Chase brings 6+ years of clinical experience to FUEL. He specializes in cranial, visceral, and structural osteopathy, fascial work, and autonomic nervous system regulation — working with trauma, athletic, and hospice populations with equal care and precision.

Jake Collier

Registered Massage Therapist
RMT · NASM CPT · IASTM Level 1

Originally from Manchester, UK, Jake relocated to Canada in 2016 bringing a background as a personal trainer. He specializes in assisted stretching and soft tissue therapy, and holds a firm belief that "care should feel collaborative, supportive, and empowering." Enjoys camping, nature, travel, and live music.

Zoe Cooper

Doctor of Acupuncture & TCM Practitioner
DAc · TCM Practitioner · Grant MacEwan

Zoe treats holistically — mind, body, and soul. Experienced with headaches, migraines, anxiety, depression, chronic pain, insomnia, and facial paralysis, she offers acupuncture, gua-sha, cupping, tui-na, and electro-acupuncture. At home she shares her life with 2 cats and 2 dogs, and has a deep love for 80s films.

Noreen Remtulla

Clinic Administrator
Corporate Healthcare Background

Noreen transitioned from a corporate healthcare background to become the heart of FUEL's front office. She coordinates appointments and patient inquiries with warmth and efficiency. A dedicated community advocate, Noreen is a 30-year Girl Guide member and sits on the United Way Alberta Capital Region cabinet. She loves river valley walks and local food events.
