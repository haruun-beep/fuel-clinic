What Is B-Pulse Therapy?

The B-Pulse chair is a revolutionary non-invasive pelvic floor treatment that uses Pulsed Electromagnetic Fields (PEMF) to activate, strengthen, and rehabilitate the pelvic floor and surrounding lumbar musculature — all while you remain fully clothed, seated comfortably in the chair.

PEMF technology delivers precisely calibrated electromagnetic pulses through the seat of the chair, stimulating thousands of supramaximal pelvic floor contractions per session. This is equivalent to performing thousands of Kegel exercises in a single 30-minute treatment — far beyond what voluntary muscle contraction alone can achieve.

Sessions are painless, require no preparation, and involve no disrobing whatsoever. Most patients read, use their phone, or simply relax during treatment.

Benefits for Women

Bladder Control

Strengthens the pelvic floor muscles responsible for urinary continence — reducing stress incontinence, urgency, and leakage during exercise, laughing, or sneezing.

Overactive Bladder & UTI Support

Reduces urinary urgency and frequency. PEMF therapy also supports a healthier pelvic environment that may reduce susceptibility to urinary tract infections.

Libido & Sexual Health

Improves blood flow and nerve sensitivity in the pelvic region, enhancing arousal, sensation, and sexual satisfaction.

Endometriosis Support

Anti-inflammatory electromagnetic stimulation may help reduce pelvic pain and inflammation associated with endometriosis.

Postpartum Recovery

Safely and effectively rebuilds pelvic floor strength following vaginal or caesarean delivery — addressing weakness, prolapse risk, and diastasis recti.

Menopause Support

Addresses pelvic floor laxity and atrophy associated with declining estrogen — reducing incontinence and improving quality of life during and after menopause.

Benefits for Men

Bladder Control

Strengthens the pelvic floor muscles that support the urethra and bladder — reducing urgency, frequency, and post-void dribbling.

Testosterone & Hormonal Support

PEMF stimulation of pelvic structures may support healthy testosterone production and hormonal balance.

Prostate Health

Pelvic floor strengthening and improved circulation in the prostate region may reduce benign prostatic hyperplasia (BPH) symptoms and support overall prostate health.

Erectile Function

Improved pelvic blood flow and stronger ischiocavernosus and bulbocavernosus muscles support erectile function and rigidity.

Premature Ejaculation

Strengthening the bulbocavernosus muscle through B-Pulse therapy improves voluntary ejaculatory control.

Post-Surgical Recovery

Accelerates pelvic floor recovery following prostatectomy or other pelvic surgeries — reducing incontinence and restoring function more quickly.
