Evidence-Based Approaches

Therapy Methods We Use

Cognitive Behavioural Therapy (CBT)

Identify and change negative thought patterns that drive anxiety, depression, and unhelpful behaviours.

EMDR Therapy

Eye Movement Desensitization and Reprocessing — highly effective for trauma, PTSD, and distressing memories.

Trauma-Informed Care

A compassionate, safety-first approach that recognizes and responds to the impact of trauma on your wellbeing.

Dialectical Behaviour Therapy (DBT)

Build emotional regulation, distress tolerance, and interpersonal skills for lasting mental health improvement.

Acceptance & Commitment Therapy (ACT)

Develop psychological flexibility — accept what you can't change and commit to actions aligned with your values.

Solution-Focused Therapy

Goal-oriented sessions that focus on solutions rather than dwelling on problems. Efficient and empowering.
