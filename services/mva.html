Comprehensive MVA Recovery at FUEL

A motor vehicle accident can leave lasting physical and psychological effects — even when collisions appear minor. Whiplash, soft tissue injuries, headaches, stiffness, and anxiety are among the most common presentations, and without proper treatment, symptoms can become chronic and debilitating.

At FUEL, our interdisciplinary team is experienced in providing comprehensive, evidence-based MVA care. We coordinate across chiropractic, osteopathy, massage therapy, acupuncture, and psychology to deliver individualized treatment plans that address the full complexity of accident-related injury and recovery.

$20 off your first visit for new MVA patients. Insurance coordination and direct billing available.

What MVA Care at FUEL Includes

Thorough Assessment

A comprehensive initial evaluation documenting your injuries, pain levels, functional limitations, and health history — providing the clinical foundation for your care plan and insurance documentation.

Active Rehabilitation

Structured rehabilitative exercise to restore strength, stability, and neuromuscular control — helping you rebuild confidence in your body's ability to move safely.

Manual Therapy

Chiropractic adjustments, osteopathic treatment, and registered massage therapy targeting the specific tissues affected by your accident — reducing pain, stiffness, and inflammation.

Insurance & Claims Guidance

Our experienced administrative team assists you in navigating insurance claims, communicating with adjusters, and ensuring your treatment is properly documented for coverage purposes.

Educational Support

We help you understand your injuries, what to expect during recovery, and how to manage symptoms at home — empowering you to actively participate in your healing.

Psychological Support

MVA-related anxiety, trauma, and PTSD are real and treatable. Christina Worthing provides integrated psychological care for patients whose recovery involves emotional as well as physical dimensions.

Conditions Treated

  • Whiplash and cervical spine injuries
  • Soft tissue injuries (muscles, ligaments, tendons, fascia)
  • Post-concussion headaches and migraines
  • Back and thoracic spine pain
  • Shoulder and upper extremity injuries
  • Hip, knee, and lower extremity injuries
  • Nerve pain and radiculopathy
  • Dizziness and vertigo following head impact
  • Anxiety, PTSD, and driving-related fear
  • Sleep disruption and fatigue

What to Expect After Treatment

  • Significant reduction in pain and stiffness
  • Improved range of motion and physical function
  • Clear understanding of your injuries and recovery timeline
  • Confidence returning to daily activities, driving, and work
  • Proper documentation to support your insurance claim

Your 4-Step MVA Process at FUEL

Step 1 — Notify Your Insurer

Contact your auto insurance adjuster and inform them of your intent to seek treatment for injuries sustained in the accident. Obtain your claim number.

Step 2 — Book at FUEL

Book your initial assessment with Dr. Saša Babović. Call us at (780) 705-5452 or book online. Mention that you are an MVA patient.

Step 3 — Share Adjuster Details

Bring your insurance adjuster's contact information and claim number to your first appointment. Our front desk team will handle the communication from there.

Step 4 — Begin Treatment

Your treatment begins while we await insurance approval. Coverage is typically applied retroactively to your first session once approved. You pay $20 less on your first visit.
