Owner & Founder

Brandon Barr

Manual Osteopathic Therapist
MOT · RMT · PTS

A former collegiate elite athlete, Brandon has spent nearly two decades integrating therapies to reduce recovery time. Trained at Grant MacEwan University and the National Manual Osteopathic College of Canada, he is currently pursuing a Graduate Certificate in Pain Management at the University of Alberta — ensuring his practice stays grounded in the latest pain science research.

Brandon's philosophy is simple: treat the whole person, not just the complaint. His approach integrates osteopathic manual therapy, massage, and movement education into cohesive, patient-centred care plans. Outside the clinic, you'll find him playing baseball, hockey, golf, or walking his dog.

Chiropractic Care

Saša (Sasha) Babović

Doctor of Chiropractic
BSc Kinesiology · DC · MSc Sports Medicine

A former youth sports coach with deep biomechanics expertise, Sasha graduated from Simon Fraser University before completing his Doctor of Chiropractic and a Master of Science in Sports Medicine from the University of Western States. His rare combination of kinesiology, chiropractic, and sports medicine training allows him to address the full spectrum of musculoskeletal conditions.

He emphasizes patient education and individualized care through collaborative, multi-angle treatment approaches. Enjoys sports, golf, gaming, and the sauna.

Psychology & Counselling

Christina Worthing

Registered Psychologist
Registered Psychologist · Biopsychosocial Model

Christina specializes in pain management through the biopsychosocial model — understanding that pain is never purely physical. Her expertise was sharpened through years of counselling individuals dealing with workplace injuries, giving her a nuanced, deeply practical understanding of how chronic pain, identity, and mental health intersect.

Trained in ACT, CBT, Solution-Focused, and Emotion-Focused therapy, she addresses anxiety, stress, depression, grief, relationships, sleep, and chronic pain. Outside the clinic, Christina plays music at church, cycles, hikes, and plays board games.

ACT Therapy CBT Chronic Pain Trauma & PTSD

Acupuncture & TCM

Zihao (Albert) Wang

Doctor of Acupuncture & RMT
DAc · TCM Practitioner · RMT

Albert combines traditional Chinese medicine techniques with contemporary approaches to deliver holistic care. His dual designation as a Doctor of Acupuncture and Registered Massage Therapist gives him a uniquely integrated toolkit — merging TCM diagnostic precision with hands-on massage expertise for comprehensive whole-body care.

Fluent in English and Mandarin, Albert offers needling, cupping, moxibustion, ear seed therapy, and Tui Na massage — tailoring each session to the individual. His bilingual practice makes quality TCM care accessible to a wider Edmonton community.

Acupuncture TCM Massage Therapy Bilingual (EN/中文)

Osteopathy & Massage

Chase Dach

International Osteopath & RMT
DOMP · RMT

With European osteopathy training and MacEwan massage therapy credentials, Chase brings 6+ years of clinical experience to FUEL. He specializes in cranial, visceral, and structural osteopathy, fascial work, and autonomic nervous system regulation — a rare combination that allows him to address deeply held patterns of tension and dysfunction.

Chase has worked with trauma, athletic, and hospice populations with equal care and precision — adapting his approach to meet each patient exactly where they are. His calm, thoughtful presence is as much a part of his treatment as his technical skill.

Osteopathy Craniosacral Visceral Manipulation Fascial Work

Massage Therapy

Jake Collier

Registered Massage Therapist
RMT · NASM CPT · IASTM Level 1

Originally from Manchester, UK, Jake relocated to Canada in 2016 bringing a strong background as a certified personal trainer. His dual expertise in massage therapy and exercise rehabilitation allows him to bridge the gap between hands-on treatment and movement-based recovery — making him a natural partner for patients working through injury.

Jake specializes in assisted stretching, soft tissue therapy, and IASTM (instrument-assisted soft tissue mobilization). He holds a firm belief that "care should feel collaborative, supportive, and empowering." Enjoys camping, nature, travel, and live music.

Massage Therapy IASTM Personal Training Soft Tissue

Acupuncture & TCM

Zoe Cooper

Doctor of Acupuncture & TCM Practitioner
DAc · TCM Practitioner · Grant MacEwan

Zoe treats holistically — mind, body, and soul. Trained at Grant MacEwan University, she brings a thoughtful, patient-centred approach to every session. Her areas of expertise include headaches and migraines, anxiety, depression, chronic pain, insomnia, facial paralysis, and women's health conditions.

Zoe's full TCM toolkit includes acupuncture, gua-sha, cupping, tui-na, and electro-acupuncture — blending ancient tradition with modern clinical evidence. At home she shares her life with 2 cats and 2 dogs, and has a deep love for 80s films.
